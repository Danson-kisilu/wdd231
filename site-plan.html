🍽️ Nairobi Street Kitchen

Site Plan – Local Food Vendor Directory

📛 Site Name

Nairobi Street Kitchen

This name represents a directory focused on street food vendors in Nairobi, Kenya. It is memorable, easy to spell, and captures the vibrant food culture of the city.

Optional domain: nairo bistro.com

🎯 Site Purpose

The site provides a comprehensive directory of street food vendors and food trucks in Nairobi. Users can discover vendors by location, cuisine, and price range, view ratings and hours, and find weekly spotlights.

💬 User Scenarios

  • "What are the top-rated food trucks near Kenyatta Market?" – Location-based search with ratings.
  • "Which vendors offer vegetarian or vegan options?" – Dietary filtering.
  • "How can I get my food business listed on this site?" – Vendor submission information.

🎨 Color Scheme

  • Deep Green (#2d6a4f) – Headers, footer, section headings
  • Warm Orange (#f4a261) – Accent borders, hover effects
  • Soft Cream (#fefae0) – Page background
  • Dark Green (#1b4332) – Body text

🔤 Typography

Primary Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif

  • Headings: Open Sans, weight 700, Deep Green
  • Body text: Open Sans, weight 400, Dark Green
  • Buttons/Links: Open Sans, weight 600, Orange accent on hover
